About Us

Welcome to RentMotion

At 2024, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch car rental services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. With our headquarters based in the bustling city of Šiauliai, Lithuania, and divisions strategically located at Vilnius Airport, Kaunas Airport, and Riga Airport in Latvia, we are committed to delivering convenience, reliability, and exceptional customer service across the region.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, RentMotion started with a simple mission: to make car rental easy, affordable, and accessible for everyone. Over the years, we have grown from a small local business to a leading car rental provider in Lithuania and Latvia. Our expansion is driven by our dedication to quality and our ability to adapt to the changing needs of our customers.

Our Locations

Šiauliai Headquarters

Located in the heart of Šiauliai, our main headquarters is the operational backbone of our company. This is where we manage our fleet, coordinate services across all branches, and ensure that our high standards of service are consistently met.

Vilnius Airport Division

Our Vilnius Airport branch serves as a convenient pick-up and drop-off point for travelers arriving in Lithuania's capital. We offer a wide range of vehicles, from compact cars for city drives to larger vehicles for family trips, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free rental experience right from the airport.

Kaunas Airport Division

Strategically located at Kaunas Airport, this division caters to the needs of travelers visiting Lithuania's second-largest city. Our Kaunas branch provides a diverse selection of vehicles, ensuring that every customer finds the perfect car for their journey.

Riga Airport Division

Expanding our services to Latvia, our Riga Airport branch is dedicated to providing the same high-quality car rental experience to travelers arriving in Riga. With a comprehensive fleet and a team of friendly professionals, we ensure that your car rental process is as seamless as possible.

Our Fleet

At RentMotion, we offer an extensive range of vehicles to meet every need and preference. From economical compact cars to luxurious sedans, spacious SUVs, and specialized vehicles like motorcycles and construction equipment, our fleet is regularly updated and meticulously maintained to ensure safety, comfort, and reliability.

Our Commitment

We are committed to making your car rental experience exceptional. Our core values of reliability, convenience, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. Whether you are renting a car for business, leisure, or any other purpose, we aim to provide you with a vehicle that suits your needs perfectly and a service experience that exceeds your expectations.
